User Manual & Scientific References

This tool assesses 8 dimensions of relationship dynamics using 30 items (0‑5 scale). Probabilities are computed via weighted softmax based on research‑informed weights. Recommendations include:

  • Main hypotheses with severity levels (🟢 mild, 🟠 moderate, 🔴 high).
  • Combination patterns when two hypotheses exceed 30%.
  • Extreme dimension scores (📈 >80%, 📉 <20%).

Key references:

  • Bowlby, J. (1969). Attachment and Loss.
  • Ainsworth, M. (1978). Patterns of Attachment.
  • Mikulincer, M. & Shaver, P.R. (2007). Attachment in Adulthood.
  • Gottman, J. (1994). Why Marriages Succeed or Fail.
  • Hutchison, C. (2024). Romantic Relationship Boundaries Scales (RRBS).
  • Rusbult, C.E. et al. (2011). The Investment Model of Commitment.
  • Alexander, R. et al. (2001). ASQ‑SF validation.

Interpret with caution – relationships are complex and require longitudinal observation.
